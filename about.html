"I do not enjoy being an activist, but given that my existence is so often politicized, the creative content and professional work that I naturally gravitate toward often seems to be considered activism."

When I make art for myself, it is always in an effort to get to know myself more deeply.

To make art for others, I need to get to know them on a human level.

Raised by the Entertainment Capital of the World in a family with Mexica, Tepehuano, Afro-Mexican, and Spanish roots; an eternal practical optimist in the face of every obstacle; an academic-turned-artist from Duke University to Lesley University, I can't wait to make something with you.

- Adi
